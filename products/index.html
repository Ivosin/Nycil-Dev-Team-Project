Alkyd Resins
An alkyd is a polyester resin modified by the addition of fatty acids and other components. Alkyds are used in paints, varnishes and in moulds for casting.
Learn More
Polyamide resins
Synthetic polyamides are commonly used in textiles, automotive industry, carpets, kitchen utensils and sportswear due to their high durability and strength
Learn More
Polyester
Polyester resins are used for flat roofs, pipes, storage tanks and other waterproofing linings as well as high-specification components for the construction, marine, aerospace and automotive industries.
Learn More
Driers, Additives, Thinner
The main function is to accelerate the drying of resin (mainly alkyd) in the paint and in consequence paint film becomes hard.
Learn More
Emulsions
It has been widely used in the printing industry due to its excellent properties such as good hardness, luster, acid and alkali resistance, weather and pollution resistance, and nontoxicity
Learn More
Blocked Catalyst
Blocked catalysts speed up the formation of polymers from the resin. They are used in gloss paints to accelerate the polymerization of the alkyd resin.
Learn More
Thermoplastic
Thermoplastic are used to make different objects such as food packaging, waterproof clothing, non-stick pots and pans, eye-wear, CDs and DVDs, cable insulation, water bottles, LCD screens, paint, furniture, toys, roofing, and medical devices.
Learn More
Thermosetting
Thermosetting are used for gas and water pipelines, automotive parts, medical equipment, construction machinery parts, signage, storage boxes, electrical plugs and casings, kitchen appliances and toys.
Learn More
Adhesives & Thickeners
Adhesives is any non-metallic substance applied to one or both surfaces of two separate items that binds them together and resists their separation
